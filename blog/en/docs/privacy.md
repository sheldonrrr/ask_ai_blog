# Privacy

## What is Sent to AI

- Book metadata (title, author, publisher, date, language)
- Your questions
- Your persona (if enabled)
- Prompts if you customize them

## What is NOT Sent

- Your calibre tags, comments, or reviews
- Reading progress
- Personal data

## API Key Storage

- Stored locally on your computer
- Never sent to third parties
- Location: `calibre config folder/plugins/ask_ai_plugin.json`

## Maximum Privacy

For maximum privacy, use **Ollama**:

- Runs locally on your computer
- Nothing sent online
- Complete control over your data

See [Free API Options](./free-api-options#option-4-ollama-local-no-internet) for Ollama setup.
